Welcome to mentr!



mentr matches students from historically underrepresented groups to tech professionals who are willing to provide them with career-oriented guidance and encouragement





Users create a profile and specify their skills, new interests they hope to learn about, their school, and their availability. We also ask whether you're currently available to teach, learn, or both!





We then suggest matches based on shared skills/interests and availability. You can also privately specify preferences such as a woman mentor, or member of the LGBT community.





mentr provides an organized, inclusive platform for tech professionals to support STEM education and help marginalized youth see themselves in the field!
